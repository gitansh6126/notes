What ML Is NOT

Not Procedural

Real life: A tax app with an explicit rule for every income bracket.

Analogy: A recipe card — follow the exact steps, get the exact result, every time. ML instead behaves like a chef improvising from years of taste patterns, not a fixed card.

Not Memorization

Real life: A spam filter that only blocks emails it has seen word-for-word before misses new spam.

Analogy: A student who memorizes last year's exam answers verbatim — and fails this year's reworded questions.
